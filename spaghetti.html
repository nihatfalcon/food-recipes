Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200 g spaghetti
  • 200 g tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  4. Mix spaghetti with sauce and serve.
