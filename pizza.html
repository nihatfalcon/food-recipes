Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250 g flour
  • 150 ml warm water
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, water, and yeast.
  2. Let dough rise for 1 hour.
  3. Roll out dough and add toppings.
  4. Bake at 220°C for 15–20 minutes.
